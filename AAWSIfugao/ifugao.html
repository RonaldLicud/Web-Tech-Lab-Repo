Bulul

It is a carved wooden figurine to protect the rice crop of the Ibalois. It was believed that using such figurines gains power from their great ancestors. It is very important to the Ifugao people because many of them believe that from this, comes a abundant and plentiful harvest.

Pungamngan

It is an elongated wooden ritual box that is used for planting and harversting gathered rituals of the family.
